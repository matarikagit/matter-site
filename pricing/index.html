How Pricing Works

Simple levels. Clear scope.

Matter uses budget-qualified pricing. Microbudget access rates stay available for true low-budget creators, while larger productions are priced at the level their scope requires.

Level 1

Consultant

For creators who want guidance, direction, and feedback while handling execution themselves.

  • Strategy and consultation
  • Calls and communication
  • Feedback on rollout and assets
  • Tools, resources, and next steps
Level 2

Partner

For creators who want strategy plus hands-on support with execution, packaging, or asset development.

  • Everything in Consultant
  • Support with execution
  • Asset or packaging support
  • Refinement of messaging and direction
Level 3

Manager

For creators who want Matter deeply involved in building and managing the system around the project.

  • Everything in Consultant and Partner
  • Marketing or release system management
  • Asset creation handled by Matter
  • Rollout oversight and active involvement

Marketing

Campaign Support

For positioning, messaging, assets, rollout planning, and audience-building.

  • Consultant, Partner, or Manager
  • Monthly support

Distribution

Release Support

For packaging, submission planning, release direction, and platform strategy.

  • Consultant, Partner, or Manager
  • Monthly support

Pre-Matter

Script Work

For screenplay editing, writing, structure, polish, and page development.

  • Priced by page count
  • Budget-qualified per-page rate

Post-Matter

Editing Work

For music videos, shorts, and feature-length projects that need post support.

  • Priced by project type
  • Minutes help estimate scope
