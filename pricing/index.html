    How Pricing Works

    Simple levels. Clear scope.

    Level 1 Consultant

    You execute. Matter guides the strategy, direction, feedback, next steps, and decision-making.

    Level 2 Partner

    You work with Matter. Matter helps with execution, packaging, campaign direction, assets, and refinement.

    Level 3 Manager

    Matter handles the heavier lift, manages the system, and becomes more directly involved in rollout or release planning.

    Rates are budget-qualified. Microbudget access rates are reserved for projects with total budgets under $10,000. Matter may request basic budget verification before confirming access pricing.
