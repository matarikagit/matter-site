Pricing

Support at the level you need.

Marketing and distribution are tiered by involvement. Pre-Matter and Post-Matter use simpler fixed starting rates. If the project is more specific than that, Matter can quote it directly.

Marketing Distribution Fixed Rates

Marketing

Choose your level of marketing support.

From strategy only to full campaign management.

Tier 1

Consultant

$150 / month

For creators who want guidance, direction, and feedback while handling execution themselves.

  • Strategy and consultation
  • Calls and communication
  • Feedback on rollout and assets
  • Tools, resources, and next steps

You execute. Matter guides.

Tier 2

Partner

$250 / month

For creators who want strategy plus hands-on support with execution and asset development.

  • Everything in Consultant
  • Support with execution
  • Assist with asset creation
  • Refinement of messaging and campaign direction

You work with Matter. Matter works with you.

Tier 3

Manager

$500 / month

For creators who want Matter to handle the heavy lift and build the marketing system around the project.

  • Everything in Consultant and Partner
  • Full marketing system management
  • Asset creation handled by Matter
  • Campaign planning and rollout oversight

Matter executes. You stay focused on the work.
