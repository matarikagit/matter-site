About the project

A cross-country road story about the family you choose.

Logline

Buried trauma. Fragile connection. A dangerous past.

After the death of his mother, an isolated young man joins a cross-country road trip with a former drug dealer, where buried trauma, fragile connections, and a dangerous past begin to close in.

World Episodes

Synopsis

Juniper and Cherry run toward something neither can name.

After the death of his mother, an emotionally withdrawn young man finds himself pulled into a cross-country road trip with Cherry, a former drug dealer trying to outrun her past. As they move further from home, buried trauma and unspoken truths begin to surface, testing the limits of their connection.

But when a violent figure resurfaces, their journey toward healing becomes a fight to hold onto the only sense of family they’ve found.

  • Format: animated limited series.
  • Scope: 6 episodes, 20-35 minutes each.
  • Genre: romance / drama with road trip and coming-of-age elements.
  • Tone: grounded, intimate, existential, human.

World / Tone

Grounded animation with a live-action pulse.

World

Present-day America.

Juniper unfolds across a grounded cross-country journey from New York to California.

Visual Style

Real world, hand drawn.

The style combines real-world photography with simplified hand-drawn characters.

Comparables

Themes

Grief and chosen family.

The series centers loss, identity, connection, generational trauma, and the family people choose.
