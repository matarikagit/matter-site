Overview

After the death of his mother, an isolated young man joins a cross-country road trip with a former drug dealer, where buried trauma, fragile connections, and a dangerous past begin to close in.

Romance / Drama Road Trip Coming of Age Grief Chosen Family Grounded Animation

Storyline

Juniper follows an emotionally withdrawn young man pulled into a cross-country road trip with Cherry, a former drug dealer trying to outrun her past. As the road stretches from home, old wounds and unspoken truths begin to surface. Their journey toward healing becomes a fight to hold onto the only sense of family they have found.
