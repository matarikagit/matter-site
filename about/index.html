Philosophy

The story is the strategy.

Before assets. Before rollout.

Most projects do not only need more promotion. They need a clearer emotional reason for people to pay attention.

Matter looks at the people, the process, the work itself, and the ways an audience can participate before building toward release.

The 4 P’s

A framework for project health.

01

People

The human heart behind the project. Who made it, who it speaks to, and why it matters now.

02

Process

The creation story. The decisions, challenges, breakthroughs, and context that make the work feel alive.

03

Product

The finished presentation. The trailer, poster, cut, copy, visuals, and materials that shape first impressions.

04

Participation

The audience pathway. How people discover the project, engage with it, share it, attend it, or support it.

Founder

Charlie Cooper

Filmmaker / Editor / Creative Strategist

Charlie Cooper is a Philadelphia-based filmmaker, editor, and creative professional whose work is shaped by storytelling, emotion, and human connection.

His background spans video production, editing, creative direction, social media, writing, music, and independent filmmaking. Matter grew from that mix: a company built for creators who need their work understood, not just advertised.
